Instant Porridge

Porridge is a fantastic option for those who seek to have a nutrient-rich and delicious breakfast.

Made from oats, fruits (most commonly banana and apple cut in pieces), as well as blueberries, honey and sugar, porridge provides a high amount of calories, fiber, protein and carbohidrates.

It also allows for a quick, easily prepared dish that fits perfectly for those who don't have much time between getting up and having to leave to work.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Blueberries
  • Honey
  • Almond milk
  • Banana
  • Brown sugar

Steps

  1. Put the oats in water for 12hs
  2. Cut the fruits you want to add
  3. Start cooking the oats
  4. When water starts to chirp, add the cut fruits and sugar
  5. Stir continously until the waters evaporates
